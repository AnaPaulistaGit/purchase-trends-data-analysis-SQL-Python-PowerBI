# 👨🏻‍💻 Project to analyze data on customer purchasing behavior.

This project demonstrates a complete data analysis workflow developed to replicate real-world tasks performed by analysts in professional environments.

It covers the critical steps of the analytical process, including data preparation, modeling, insight generation, visualization, and reporting.

The goal is to demonstrate practical analytical skills and the ability to transform raw data into clear insights that support well-informed decisions.


## 📌 Project Overview

The goal of this project is to simulate a complete, enterprise-level data analytics workflow, demonstrating the ability to translate raw data into strategic business intelligence through:

✅ Data Preparation,Modeling & Exploratory Data Analysis (Python): Clean and transform the raw dataset for analysis.

✅ Data Analysis (SQL): Simulate business transactions, and run queries to extract insights on customer segments, loyalty, and purchase drivers.

✅ Visualization & Insights (Power BI): Build an interactive dashboard that highlights key patterns and trends, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven decisions.

✅ Report and Presentation: Write a clear project report summarizing your key findings and business recommendations. Prepare a presentation that visually communicates insights and actionable recommendations to stakeholders.


## Business Problem

A major retail company seeks to obtain more detailed information about customer purchasing behavior in order to improve sales performance, increase customer satisfaction, and strengthen long-term loyalty.

In recent months, the management team has observed notable changes in purchasing patterns across different customer profiles, product categories, and sales channels, including online and offline transactions. These changes suggest that customer preferences and decision-making processes may be influenced by several factors that are not yet fully understood.

Specifically, the company wants to identify how variables such as discounts, product reviews, seasonal trends, and payment preferences influence consumer purchasing decisions and repeat buying behavior.

Understanding these patterns is essential for enabling the company to refine its marketing strategies, optimize product offerings, and improve customer engagement initiatives.

<b>Therefore, the central business question guiding this analysis is:</b>

How can the company leverage consumer shopping data to identify purchasing trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?

By analyzing the available consumer behavior dataset, this project aims to uncover meaningful insights that can support data-driven decision-making and strategic business improvements.

## Key Analytical Questions

<b>1. Customer Segmentation</b>

Which customer segments generate the highest revenue and purchase frequency?
Understanding the most valuable customer groups can help the company design more targeted marketing strategies and loyalty programs.

<b>2. Purchase Drivers</b>

How do factors such as discounts, product reviews, and seasonal trends influence purchasing behavior?
Identifying these drivers can help the company optimize pricing strategies and promotional campaigns.

<b>3. Channel Performance</b>

How do online and offline sales channels differ in terms of revenue, transaction volume, and customer engagement?
This analysis can help the company allocate resources more effectively between digital and physical channels.

4. Product Category Performance

Which product categories contribute the most to total sales and customer retention?
This insight can guide product assortment decisions and inventory management.

5. Customer Loyalty and Repeat Purchases

What factors are associated with repeat purchases and long-term customer loyalty?
Understanding these patterns can help the company strengthen retention strategies and increase customer lifetime value.


## Data Analytics Workflow

The project follows a structured analytics pipeline:

1. Business Problem Definition

Identify the key business questions and define the metrics required for analysis.

2. Data Ingestion and Cleaning (Python)

Raw datasets are imported into Python where data cleaning and preprocessing are performed.

3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

EDA techniques are applied to understand data distributions, detect anomalies, and uncover patterns.

4. Data Storage (SQL Database)

Cleaned datasets are stored in a relational SQL database to enable efficient querying.

5. Analytical Queries (SQL)

SQL is used to perform aggregations and generate datasets for business intelligence analysis.

6. Data Visualization (Power BI)

Interactive dashboards are created to visualize KPIs and business performance indicators.

7. Insight Generation

Key findings and patterns are summarized in an analytical report.

8. Stakeholder Presentation

Insights are presented using a structured business presentation.

9. Documentation and Version Control

All project files are documented and version-controlled using GitHub.
